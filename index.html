Where are #mbfw Tweeters From?

The New York Fashion Week just finished up this week. Here, we look at the origin cities of the people talking about this event on Twitter. This visual shows the locations of Twitter users responsible for nearly 30,000 tweets containing the #mbfw hashtag.

Top Cities

For tweets from users with a particular city listed, here are the top cities of users tweeting about #mbfw. Most of the fashion capitols are present, however, notably absent is Paris - which has less then 100 tweets.
